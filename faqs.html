Health and Safety

While our all-natural products are generally safe, we advise individuals with specific health conditions, pregnant or breastfeeding women, and those on medication to consult with a healthcare professional before using our supplements. Our products are intended to support wellness, not to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

If you have a known mushroom allergy, it is crucial to avoid our products. Always consult with a healthcare provider if you have any concerns about allergies or sensitivities.
